About Me

Hello! My name is Mason Maile, and I am a junior mechanical engineering student at MSOE, also minoring in electrical engineering. People often say that when you do what you love, you never work a day in your life, and that’s exactly what I’m chasing. For me, engineering doesn’t feel like work at all, it’s something I genuinely love. Whether it’s designing, building, or problem-solving, I find myself fully immersed in the process.

Looking ahead, my goal is to work for a company that mirrors this same passion. I want to be part of a team that creates products people notice, things that make someone stop and say, “That’s cool.” More importantly, I want to help design products that actually improve the lives of their users. To me, that’s what engineering is all about: combining innovation, impact, and creativity in a way that truly makes a difference.

My Approach To Engineering

I’ve come to realize that the true joy of engineering lies less in the destination and more in the journey. The late nights spent problem-solving, the roadblocks that force creative solutions, and the small breakthroughs along the way are what give every project its meaning. Each challenge isn’t just an obstacle to overcome but an opportunity to grow, and it’s that process that makes engineering so rewarding to me.

The projects above reflect this mindset. Every one of them pushed me in new ways, teaching me lessons that went far beyond the technical side. They don’t just show what I built, they capture the experiences, the problem-solving, and the growth that came with creating something meaningful.
